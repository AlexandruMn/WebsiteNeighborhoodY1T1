The St. Bavo Church

In the middle of the Grote Market, stands tall the Saint Bavo Church. This cathedral is a very old and beautiful building that has been around for a long time. It has a tall, pointed tower and a gorgeous facade, testifying to the skill of the people who built it long ago. Whenever you walk through Bakenes, you'll always see the cathedral. It's a big part of the neighborhood's history and importance.

Art and Culture

Bakenes isn't just about old stuff. It is also a vibrant place of creativity and culture. Our neighborhood supports local artists and is full of small shops, art spaces, and cozy cafes where local talent shines. Whether you want to see unique art, purchase handmade items, or enjoy delicious coffee, Bakenes has something for every art lover.
